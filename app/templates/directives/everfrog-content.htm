What You Do

As a data scientist, you will help analyze the data we gather at EverFrog to make statistical changes to our core infrastructure and services. You will be the one to determine what we, both as a team and corporation, should do to best improve our overall image.

What we expect from you as an EverFrog Scientist

Here at EverFrog, we take our recruitmnet process very seriously. We believe that a person must have exemplary skills as well as be both fun and nice to work with overall. Keeping that in mind, we also require that you be fluent in statistics and other forms of math.

Additionally, you will need to be experienced with at least two of the following:

  • SQL / PostgreSQL
  • R / Python
  • Scala / Haskell
